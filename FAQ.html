Frequently Asked Questions

What's the bag limit for Economy class both Carry-on and Check-In?
The bag limit should be 7kg Carry-on for economy class, and 23kg for check in. Each passenger is allowed 23kgx2 and 7kgx1.
What happens if the airline loses my baggage?
If you’re at the airport, please speak to a member of the FlyAfrica team for help. If you’ve left the airport, please complete our Lost Property FormLost property form to report missing personal items or cabin baggage and we’ll respond promptly.
What should I do if my baggage is delayed?
If you can't find your baggage at the terminal, you should visit the baggage services desk at the airport. This is usually found in the customs hall near the baggage claim area. When we receive your baggage, we’ll contact you to arrange a delivery time, subject to approval by customs.
