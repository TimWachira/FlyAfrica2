SkyRewards

Join FlyAfrica's SkyRewards and enjoy exclusive benefits when you fly with FlyAfrica. Earn miles every time you travel and redeem them for rewards.

  • Access to exclusive promotions and discounts
  • Priority boarding
  • Complimentary upgrades
  • Free checked baggage

Tiers

Our loyalty program offers three tiers: Eagle, Vulture, and Hawk. Progress through the tiers to unlock additional benefits.
